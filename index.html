How We Met

Connor and I met in high school when Connor and his family moved to Waunakee in 2010. We were in the same friend group and got to know each other very well through a cabin trip our senior year. A few years went by and I found myself single and back in Waunakee for the summer while working at the Aldo Leopold Nature Center. During that time, Connor and I began to spend a lot more time together and started to see each other frequently. On Halloween night, we kissed and I left him with the romantic words of “don’t make this weird”. But he did make it weird and I am glad he did. Connor and I began to spend more intentional time together and realizing that we had a lot in common and were a pretty good fit.

Around January of 2015, we officially started dating. We had a long distance relationship while I was in school at the University of Wisconsin- Stevens Point and Connor was in Madison working. In 2016, Connor decided to spend a year in Stevens Point while attending Mid State Technical College for business. We made a lot of great friends that year and learned what it was like to be a couple living in the same area. This year, Connor returned to Madison to work full time while I finished up my final year at University.

On December 10th, while home for the weekend, Connor said he wanted to spend some time together and go for a walk at Indian Lake Park to take some pictures. It was an absolutely gorgeous day with a recent snowfall covering the landscape in a nice fresh blanket of white. I enjoyed our walk with a slight awareness that this might be ‘the moment’. When he set up the camera to take the picture, we posed and the camera flashed like normal. This threw me off my guard and I began thinking; ‘oh well it is just normal picture’. We took one more picture that turned out pretty similar to the first. On the third picture, he got down on one knee presenting me with the ring and asked me to be his wife. I said yes, through some happy tears. We are so excited to spend the rest of our lives together and are so happy that you can join us in celebrating our day!
